Summer Outfits


During the summer time, it's really hot so lots of people wear shorts, tank tops, t-shirts, open shoes and running shoes. This way they'll be able to stay cool during the hot temperatures.



Casual


A casual outfit during the summer would be a pair of shots either sporty or jean paired with a short sleeve to and some sneakers.

Formal


A formal outfit for the summer would be a pair of jean shorts with a nice top or a romper or dress with either a pair of heels or a sandals accompanied by some jewelry.

Active


Since it can be very hot during the summer, most outfits worn to be active consist of shorts, t-shirts or sleeveless shirts, running shoes, a cap and having your hair up in a ponytail.
