The Year's Closet

The Year’s Closet is a website that can help people choose an outfit if every they’re in a hurry and have no time to find an outfit or if they’re having trouble figuring out what to wear. The website contains 4 categorie of outfits. It has outfits for the winter, spring, summer and, the fall. Once you open the website you see the home page which has a log in button on the left corner where you can create your own account this way, you can be getting updates of any new outfits that are uploaded. if ever you want to find out more about this website you can check our socials on the bottom of the homepage.
